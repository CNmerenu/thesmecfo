What Sets Us Apart

Global standards. Local fluency. Your CFO.

We bring the financial discipline of global capital markets to the businesses shaping Africa's future. Every engagement is led by an ACA-qualified chartered accountant with nearly a decade of experience structuring and executing transactions at one of the world's leading financial institutions.

Institutional-grade financial leadership

Financial control, reporting infrastructure, and capital markets experience applied to every engagement. Your business gets the same calibre of financial governance that underpins multi-billion-pound decisions.

Dual-jurisdiction regulatory fluency

We operate across UK and Nigerian regulatory environments: FRS 102, IFRS, Companies House, and CAC. For businesses with cross-border operations, we navigate both systems with equal confidence and prepare the financial documents you need for every compliance obligation.

Foundation to strategy, end to end

Where no financial infrastructure exists, we build it: chart of accounts, IFRS-compliant statements, reporting frameworks, internal controls. Where the basics are in place, we provide the strategic oversight that turns numbers into insight and action.

A strategic partner, not a service provider

We sit alongside your leadership team as a long-term financial partner. We take on a limited number of engagements so every client receives our full attention, invested in your outcomes and focused on the decisions that move your business forward.

About Us

The SME CFO.
Laying the foundation for Africa's next unicorns.

The SME CFO is a CFO advisory firm built on a single conviction: that African businesses deserve the same calibre of financial leadership that powers the world's most successful companies.

We provide world-class CFO advisory services that help founders make better financial decisions, grow sustainably, and raise capital with confidence. We are building the financial infrastructure on which Africa's next generation of great businesses will scale.

Meet the Founder

Olubunmi is a Chartered Accountant and corporate finance strategist with nearly a decade of experience at one of the world's leading financial institutions, advising on treasury strategy, structuring and executing complex, high-value transactions, and managing strategic priorities across global markets.

Over the course of her career, she has executed multi-billion-pound transactions, including a £2.3 billion acquisition and a £750 million share buyback programme.

Through The SME CFO, she now partners with African and diaspora-owned businesses to build the financial systems, controls, and strategic discipline required to grow, scale sustainably, raise capital, and compete on a global stage.

African businesses deserve the same calibre of financial leadership and capital that powers Silicon Valley. We're building the bridge to make that happen.
